A flower shop, also known as a florist or floral boutique, is a retail establishment that specializes in selling fresh flowers, floral arrangements, and other floral products. These shops are often visited by individuals looking to purchase flowers for various occasions such as weddings, anniversaries, birthdays, funerals, or simply to brighten up their homes.

Online flower shops provide a convenient and accessible way for customers to order and send flowers for various occasions. They offer a wide selection of flowers, customization options, secure ordering processes, and reliable delivery services to make the experience of sending and receiving flowers a seamless one.
